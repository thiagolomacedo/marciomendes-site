CAPOEIRA ANGOLA

Preserving the Roots

Our Mission

The Capoeira Angola School seeks to rescue and preserve rare or underexplored elements in modern capoeira, maintaining the historical and cultural essence of this art.

We believe that understanding the roots of capoeira is essential for its authentic practice and transmission to future generations.

School Rules

1
Respect the Mestre and all fellow practitioners at all times
2
Mandatory uniform: black pants and yellow shirt with school logo
3
Classes are conducted in Portuguese to preserve cultural authenticity
4
Zero tolerance for lateness - punctuality is essential
5
Maximum of 15 students per class for quality instruction
6
Payment must be made on time to maintain your enrollment
7
No jewelry, watches, or accessories during practice
8
Personal hygiene is mandatory - clean uniform for every class
9
Commitment to learning the history and philosophy of capoeira
10
Represent the school with honor and integrity outside classes
