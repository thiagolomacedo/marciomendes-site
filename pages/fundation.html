Our Mission

To promote, preserve, and elevate capoeira as a cultural heritage and competitive sport on the world stage. We believe that capoeira deserves recognition alongside other martial arts in international competitions.

Through education, events, and advocacy, we work to ensure that capoeira's rich history and traditions are honored while adapting to the modern world.
