TRECIRAL Initiation

This work is the first volume of a trilogy dedicated to TRECIRAL, which extends far beyond a mere movement technique, establishing itself as a genuine philosophy of life. Through a detailed exploration of body movement, this initial book demonstrates the foundations and core components that gave rise to TRECIRAL, including a thorough description and analysis of its logo symbolism. In this first part, the reader will find a comprehensive introduction and a detailed historical context, which seek to elucidate the origins and profound meaning of the methodology. Each subsequent chapter is dedicated to a distinct component of the technique, presented progressively and in an accessible manner. The goal is to ensure the reader not only assimilates the content but also experiences a continuous developmental process, evolving harmoniously and consistently with each new stage explored.

In Mila's Eyes

Mila's Vision presents the world through the eyes of a being who loves without hesitation: a female dog observing the human species. From her perspective, the relationship between humans and animals becomes a mirrorâ€”one that reflects not only our strengths, but also our contradictions. Mila sees humans as the dominant species on the planet, gifted with intelligence, creativity, and the unique ability to shape the world according to their will. Yet this same capacity often leads to confusion, sadness, and destructive behavior. From her loyal point of view, she observes a species that believes itself superior but frequently struggles to understand the true essence of life.
