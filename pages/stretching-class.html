STRETCHING CLASS

Flexibility, mobility, and body awareness

IMG - What is Stretching

What is Stretching?

Stretching is the practice of deliberately lengthening and loosening muscles and their surrounding connective tissues to enhance flexibility, mobility, and joint range of motion.

Why Stretch? The Benefits

Integrating regular stretching into your class routine or personal practice brings numerous benefits for body and mind.

IMG - Flexibility

Improved Flexibility and Range of Motion

When muscles and joints become more supple, you'll enjoy a broader, freer range of movement.

IMG - Performance

Better Performance & Movement Quality

Stretching allows muscles to operate more smoothly and joints to move with less restriction.

IMG - Injury Prevention

Reduced Risk of Injury & Muscular Imbalance

Regular stretching helps keep muscles elongated and balanced.

IMG - Circulation

Better Circulation, Movement Efficiency & Recovery

Stretching promotes blood flow to muscles and tends to ease stiffness.

IMG - Posture

Improved Posture, Comfort and Well-Being

Tight muscles often pull joints out of alignment or restrict natural posture.

IMG - Mental Benefits

Mental and Emotional Benefits

Stretching gives you a chance to slow down, breathe, and connect with your body.

About the Instructor

IMG MESTRE 1

Mestre Márcio Mendes

Mestre Márcio Mendes is much more than a capoeira master — he is an educator, thinker, and a catalyst for social change.

He founded TRECIRAL, an innovative technique that merges capoeira movements with deep stretching, emotology, meditation, and Afro-Brazilian dance.

IMG MESTRE 2

A philosophy and biology student, he offers insightful analyses of human behavior from social, political, and emotional perspectives through blog articles and YouTube content.

In Toronto, he founded two capoeira schools, each offering a distinct style tailored to the unique needs and preferences of its students. As president of the International Capoeira Federation, Mestre Mendes is at the forefront of the global movement to have capoeira recognized as an Olympic sport.

Beyond his public achievements, Mestre Márcio is an unwavering source of inspiration in our lives. Growing up with his guidance has given us a broader, more compassionate view of the world. His constant support, encouragement, and wisdom have shaped who we are. We are endlessly grateful to have such a role model — a father whose presence and principles continue to guide and uplift us every day.
