Movement, Meditation & Emotional Intelligence

IMG TRECIRAL

What is TRECIRAL?

TRECIRAL is an innovative philosophical and physical practice that combines movement, meditation, and emotology (the study of emotions), grounded in elements of capoeira.

The central focus is developing intuition as a trainable skill, aligned with thinkers like Plato and Immanuel Kant.

The Approach

The methodology emphasizes deep perception, emotional awareness, and presence rather than linear logic. It promotes what we call "active intuition" through movements inspired by capoeira, meditation, and emotional attunement.

Unlike conventional practices that separate mind and body, TRECIRAL integrates both into a unified experience of self-discovery and growth.
