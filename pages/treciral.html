TRECIRAL

Movement, Meditation & Emotional Intelligence

What is TRECIRAL

TRECIRAL is an innovative philosophical and physical practice that integrates movement, meditation, and emotology (the study of emotion), grounded in the expressive and rhythmic foundations of capoeira. At its core, TRECIRAL is a path to developing intuition — understood as the ability to anticipate events or outcomes without relying on logical reasoning, in line with the philosophical views of Plato and Immanuel Kant.

Rather than emphasizing logic or linear thought, TRECIRAL highlights the value of deep perception, emotional awareness, and presence. Through capoeira-inspired movements, meditative states, and emotional attunement, it fosters access to what may be called active intuition — a subtle intelligence that operates beyond words or analysis.

TRECIRAL treats intuition not as a mystical gift, but as a trainable skill that can be applied strategically in all areas of life, including:

  • Decision-making
  • Artistic or intellectual creation
  • Personal and spiritual growth
  • Overcoming emotional or physical barriers
  • Achieving goals with clarity and flow

Beyond its philosophical depth, TRECIRAL offers a path of personal transformation through embodied awareness and mental stillness, aiming to harmonize action with perception. It is a living, evolving methodology — rooted in practical experience, philosophical inquiry, and somatic wisdom.
